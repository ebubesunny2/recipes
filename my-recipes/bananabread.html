Banana Bread

Banana Bread is one of those timeless, feel-good recipes that fills your kitchen with wamrth and comfort. whether you're making it to use overripe bananas or simply craving a cozy treat, this classic loaf never disappionts. Soft, moist, and full of rich banana flavour, Its perfect for breakfast, snacking or sharing with friends

INGREDIENTS

  • Three ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar(can reduce to 1/2 cup if desired)
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tea spoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 11/2 cups all purpose flour

Optional Add ins

  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • A sprinkle of sugar or oats on top before baking

Steps To Prepare Banana Bread(with chocolate chips)

  1. Preheat your oven

    2. set your oven to 350°F. Grease a 9x5- inch loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.

  2. Mash the Bananas

    3. In a large bowl, mix 3 ripe bananas with a fork till smooth

  3. Add wet ingredients

    4. Stir in 1/3 cup of melted butter,3/4 cup of sugar, 1 beaten egg, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. mix until everthing is well combined

  4. Add dry ingredients

    5. Sprinkle 1 teaspon of baking soda and a pinch of salt over the mixture. Stir gently. then add 11/2 cups of all-purpose flour and mix just until the flour disappears-don't overmix.

  5. Fold in chocolate chips

    6. Add 1/2 cup of chocolate chips (or more if you like it exta chocolatey!). Fold them evenly into the batter.

  6. Pour into loaf pan

    7. Transfer the batter to the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top.

  7. Bake

    8. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean(a few melted chocolate smudges are fine).

  8. Cool and serve

    9. Let the banana bread cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
